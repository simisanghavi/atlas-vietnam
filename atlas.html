Vietnam (Viet Nam) 

14.0584N, 108.2772E 

The Socialist Republic of Vietnam, widely known as Vietnam, is not an unfamiliar country in the world. The country is the land that speaks volumes. Although this country was devastated by a series of wars that caused serious damages in the past, Vietnam is inviting and peaceful. It located in the eastern Indochina Peninsula in Southeast Asia, with Hanoi as its capital. With an area of 331,690 sq. kilometers, to the north of Vietnam, is China, and to its west is Laos and Cambodia. It is surrounded by the Gulf of Tonkin and the East Sea to its west, and to the south is the Thailand Gulf. Vietnam is a center of trading and cultural interaction. The long coastline is an attraction for many. Having a tropical climate, Vietnam is well known for from magnificent scenery and colorful hill tribes to wide terraced fields in Red River Delta and Mekong River Delta, to majestic mountains, and white sandy beaches. Vietnam, nowadays, is one of the should-not-miss destinations in Asia.
